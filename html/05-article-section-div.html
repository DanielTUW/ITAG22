The Article Tag

think of article [HTML tag] not just as a newspaper article, or a blog post, but as an article of clothing
Bruce Lawson

The article tag is referred to as a "self-contained composition"

The Section Tag

The section tag is meant to group similar portions of your document together

The Div Tag

div, short for division, is a commonly used tag. it is meant to group elements together when no other HTML element is suitable. It's usually used to group up elements for styling purposes, given that there are semantic tags (section, article, etc) for grouping as well.
